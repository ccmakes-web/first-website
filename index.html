Welcome to Caroline Carey Makes (Ccmakes) a retrospective look at the crafts and arts I enjoy.

The following are some of the crafts that I enjoy. They allow you to develop your own artistic style even if following a pattern as you adapt the design to suit your requirements.

  • Crossstitch and Tapestry work
  • Embroidery styles: Freestyle, Crewelwork, Beadwork and Goldwork
  • Knitting
  • Sewing
  • Feltwork kits
  • Weaving on a ridgied heddle loom
  • Bobbin Lace in the styles: Torchon and Tape lace
  • Air dry clay
  • Painting with Watercolours
  • Sketching using pencil and inks

Arts and Crafts have been found to be good for our general health and wellbeing.

  • All round exercise for the brain
  • Better sleep
  • Social Benefits
  • Good with (and for) your hands in promoting dexterity
  • Stress relief (lowers blood pressure)
  • Self-esteem
  • Anxiety and depression
  • Improves quality of life for those with illness
